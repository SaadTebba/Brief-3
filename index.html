Timeline

This is how our daily tours goes.

10:00 AM

Participants can arrive starting from 10 AM to get prepared for the ride. For those who have arrived earlier, there will be a special area near the gates for snacks and coffee. If you arrive by car or by any other means of transportation please leave it at the temporal parking area near the entrance. Please do not be late as the fest will start on time.

11:00 AM

The festival will start at 11 AM as soon as everyone is all set up. We will start the ride from the gates of the valley and will land near the big rock at about 12:30 PM. A small break will be followed before we will go on over the valley lake. The final landing will be at 2 PM near the forest.

03:00 AM

Now it's time to dance and relax. Everyone is welcome to join the after party which will be held in a big cozy tent at 3 PM. We have invited the best DJs of the state to make your day unforgettable with unstoppable beats.
